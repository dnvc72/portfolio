Future Career: Law

I aspire to practice law, because debate, organization, and collaboration are my key strengths. Working a job that directly helps others has always been important to me, especially because representation in fields of expertise such as law, will change America's attitude and actions towards POC and women.
The first time I envisioned myself as a lawyer was watching Legally Blonde as a little girl.


Responsibilities


  • Advise and represent clients
  • Communicate with clients, colleagues, judges, and others involved in a case
  • Conduct research and analysis of legal issues based on the region’s jurisdiction
  • Interpret laws, rulings, and regulations for individuals or businesses
  • Prepare and file legal documents, such as lawsuits, contracts, and wills



Requirements


Salary
The average salary of a lawyer is $41k-150k/year
Education
About 7 years total education is needed. A 4 year undergraduate in law, 3 year doctorate, and passing the bar exam for whatever state you plan to work in.
Traits
Traits and skills needed from a lawyer are:
  • Argumentative
  • Organized
  • Communicative
  • Confident
  • Empathic
  • Good memory recall
  • Articulate
  • Persuasive


I can see myself in this career, because I already enjoy and am successful in extracurriculars that involve the same skills and tasks as being a lawyer, such as Model UN. I also have lots of medical needs and would feel secure in a job that pays highly and will always be in demand.
