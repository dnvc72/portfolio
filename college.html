Aspiring College: UIUC

I hope to attend UIUC, because it’s in my home state, and I’ll be able to visit my family easily during the holidays. They have overseas programs for bilinguals, and I’ve visited it every year for Science Olympiad State since I was 11, and can see myself going there. It’s also achievable for me, and a realistic option without putting too much pressure on myself. At the same time, I’ll still be moving away from home and getting the classic college experience.

Info


Tuition
Tuition for in-state students is about $15,442.
Credit requirements
  • 4 years English
  • 3-3.5 years Math
  • 2 years Social Sciences
  • 2 years Lab Sciences
  • 2 years World Language
  • 2 years English
  • 2 years "Flexible Academic Units" (Electives)
Amenities
  • Hotel
  • Conference center
  • Daycare
  • Sports and recreational facilities
  • Rentable bikes & campus public transit
  • Campus police
  • Electric vehicle charging


Another College: Brown

Brown is known for its strong computer science programs, but has also produced award winning writers in all genres. I’m still a freshman and deciding what I want to pursue as an adult, so Brown is great to keep in mind as it’s a top university that would boost me in whichever path I take.

Info


Tuition
Tuition for out-of-state students is about $83,686. No specific credit requirements were found!
Amenities
  • Gyms and other athletic facilities
  • "Eateries"
  • Campus public transit
  • Extensive bookstores and libraries
  • Campus center and student lounges
  • Observatories
